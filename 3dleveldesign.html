3D Level Design Group Project

Project Description

I worked with a partner to prototype design ideas for a 3D computer game level. We had free reign to develop a level with a modern game engine with some minimal gameplay features. We opted to explore the idea of an underground world under a city opening up with mutants attacking citizens.
