The Job Adventure Freelance Project

Project Description

In summer 2013 I worked with Enable Scotland to run a workshop for young people with learning disabilities. The workshop was to show them a taster of game development. Over the course of the workshop they designed a game of their own which I then developed into a concrete, playable computer game. The project was a great success, the game was shown at a launch night where national charities and press where in attendance.
